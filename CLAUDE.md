# Moontaxi Lab

## What this site is

Moontaxi Lab is Paula McMahon's AI-native lab and workshop: a home for experiments, trainings, collaborations with small startups, and a publishing space for special public-facing projects (e.g. wedding ceremony run-of-show pages) when she needs to put something on the open web.

The site complements, but is distinct from, Paula's personal site at https://www.paulamcmahon.com/. Moontaxi Lab carries the lab work (experiments, trainings, collaborations, project artifacts); the personal site carries Paula's individual brand. Repositioned July 2026 from "advisory studio" to "lab and workshop": the Lab invites, it does not pitch. Do not reintroduce services/engagement language without Paula's direction.

## Working with Claude on this site

Standing rules:

- **Always work on a branch.** Never commit directly to `main`. Create a feature branch for any change, no matter how small.
- **Match the existing voice and tone.** Before adding or rewriting copy, read the relevant existing pages and mirror Paula's writing style. Don't introduce new voice or phrasings without checking first.
- **Ask before publishing.** Don't push to `main`, merge a PR, or trigger a deploy without explicit confirmation from Paula. Drafts and branch-level work are fine; anything that goes live is not.

## Notes for future sessions

Brand positioning between Moontaxi Lab and the personal site is actively in flux — flag any copy or structural choices that depend on positioning so they can be revisited rather than locked in.

Special projects (e.g. event run-of-show pages) may have different audiences and tone from the main lab pages. Treat them as their own thing rather than forcing consistency with the main site voice.
